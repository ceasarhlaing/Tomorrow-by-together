TXT, also known as TOMORROW X TOGETHER , is a 5-members boy group formed by Big Hit Music (part of HYBE Labels). The group consists of: Yeonjun, Soobin, Beomgyu, Taehyun and Huening Kai. They debuted on March 4th, 2019 with their first mini-album ‘The Dream Chapter: STAR’. There are no set positions within the group, it changes each comeback/song.
